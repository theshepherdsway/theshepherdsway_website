Our Team

Meet Your Guides, Your Hosts and Our Partners!

Your Guides

Fonsi

As the eldest son of a well-respected shepherd family from the region, Fonsi knows his mountains like the back of his hand. He loves meeting new people from all over the world and sharing the beauty of his beloved home country with them. Therefore, there is no guide more suited for the job!

Nina

She is a documentary photographer from a different world, but after spending much time in the mysterious "Accursed" mountains, she now sees herself as half-Malësorë. She may not be a 'true' local, but her "inside/outsider" insight provides a fresh perspective into the lives of the locals.

Your Hosts

The Bikaj's

Experience life with this energetic, bustling family of nine. They are a proud shepherd family and it's hard not to fall in love with this lively bunch. It is never a dull moment with this crew, and they are ready to welcome you into their home and lives with open arms!

The Qosaj's

A young, sweet family with four kids! They are a curious, playful bunch who love meeting travellers and learning about the world outside their own. They live in a more remote part of the mountain, but your journey to their cosy home with be well worth the effort!

The Nicaj's

Available to host German speakers soon!

Our Partners

Shtëpia e Bariut dhe Bjeshkimit

The Shepherd's Way Albania currently works with a local, Tamarë based organisation, Shtëpia e Bariut. Anthropologist and ethnologue, Martine Wolff, runs the organisation and acts as a liaison between the shepherds and the outside world. The funds we raise through our social business will be invested in projects developed by Shtëpia e Bariut, and we are happy to collaborate with them.
The Shepherd's Way Albania is a social business run by/for Malësorë shepherds, protecting an endangered cultural heritage in Kelmend, northern Albania. We aim to do this through sustainable, immersive, small-group ecotourism. Please support us and enjoy a truly unique experience!
