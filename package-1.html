Meet & Greet Day Hike

Package 1

Our Meet & Greet, Day Hike package offers you the chance to explore the beautiful surrounding areas of Lepushë together with your local guide, Fonsi. He will take you off the beaten track and give you a shepherd's insight into the landscape. Along the way, you will have the opportunity to meet other local shepherds in their summer encampments ("Stan"s), have lunch, drink coffee with them and get an authentic glimpse into their way of living.

Tours leave in the morning from Qafe Perdolec and typically take 6-8 hours. You will be escorted back to your start point before dark (typically around 4-5 pm).

For this package, there is also the option to travel on horseback for an additional fee of €50.

More Info
The Shepherd's Way Albania is a social business run by/for Malësorë shepherds, protecting an endangered cultural heritage in Kelmend, northern Albania. We aim to do this through sustainable, immersive, small-group ecotourism. Please support us and enjoy a truly unique experience!
